Mods : TerraProtection

Description

The TerraProt mod adds protection blocks. You can see details of them in the Protection Blocks page


This mod is private, but you can get free copies of it by asking Bapt-admin in Discord.

The /add_owner command was removed for players, due to compatibility between TerraProt mod and areas mod.
