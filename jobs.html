XP

You can find here the XP that you'll earn when you mine/kill/farm items or blocks

Miner

To get the XP, you need to mine the block.

Diamond (in stone) : 10 XP

Diamond (in deepslate) : 15 XP

Emerald (in stone) : 14 XP

Emerald (in deepslate) : 35 XP

Iron (in stone) : 4 XP

Iron (in deepslate) : 8 XP

Lapis (in stone) : 10 XP

Lapis (in deepslate) : 20 XP

Gold (in stone) : 6 XP

Gold (in deepslate) : 7 XP

Copper (in stone) : 2 XP

Copper (in deepslate) : 5 XP

Redstone (in stone) : 4 XP

⚠️Note : Redstone in deepslate does not give XP.
