TerraMine Rules

1. General Gameplay

  • 1.1: Using software, add-ons, or mods that alter gameplay to gain an advantage is prohibited. (Level 4 Sanction)
  • 1.2: Attacking someone's dignity, whether in-game or outside the server (e.g., after a raid), is forbidden. (Level 1-4 Sanction)
  • 1.3: Using usernames, profile pictures, or other artifacts to impersonate staff members is prohibited. (Level 2-4 Sanction)
  • 1.4: During raids, killing the same player more than 5 times is not allowed. (Level 2-4 Sanction)
  • 1.5: Killing a staff member is strictly prohibited. (Level 4 Sanction)

2. Chat and Conversations

  • 2.1: Explicit content is forbidden in both in-game chat and Discord. (Level 1-4 Sanction)
  • 2.2: Conversations meant to demean or insult a player are prohibited. (Level 1-4 Sanction)
  • 2.3: Spamming is not allowed in Discord or in-game chat. (Level 1 Sanction)
  • 2.4: Excessive use of @mentions in Discord is prohibited. (Level 2 Sanction)
  • 2.5: Advertising other servers is not allowed. (Level 1 Sanction)
  • 2.6: Insults and offensive language are forbidden. (Level 1-4 Sanction)
  • 2.7: Threatening real-life attacks is strictly prohibited. (Level 4-5 Sanction)
  • 2.8: NSFW content is not allowed. (Level 3-5 Sanction)
  • 2.9: Using provocative or offensive usernames, nicknames, or skins is forbidden. (Level 4-5 Sanction)

3. Server Stability

  • 3.1: Using any technique to lag or crash the server is prohibited. (Level 4 Sanction)
  • 3.2: Exploiting bugs or errors to simplify gameplay significantly is not allowed. (Level 4 Sanction)
  • 3.3: Damaging the spawn area is forbidden. (Level 4-5 Sanction)

4. Trading and Economy

  • 4.1: Scamming or deceiving players is strictly prohibited. (Level 3-4 Sanction)
  • 4.2: Manipulating the market for personal gain is forbidden. (Level 4 Sanction)

5. Events

  • 5.1: Cheating during events hosted by staff is not allowed. (Level 3-4 Sanction)
  • 5.2: Players must follow any specific event rules provided. (Level 1-5 Sanction)

6. Builds

  • 6.1: Griefing buildings created by staff is forbidden. (Level 4-5 Sanction)

Sanctions

  • Level 1: Mute
  • Level 2: Kick
  • Level 3: Ban for 1 minute
  • Level 4: Temporary ban (longer than 1 minute)
  • Level 5: Permanent ban

The duration of Mutes and Temporary Bans is decided by the staff.

You can appeal any Ban. Kicks and Mutes are not reversible. You can appeal in the Ban Appeal channel in our discord : TerraMine Discord
